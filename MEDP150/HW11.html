Photo Montage



The concepts I was exploring was mask layering and blending mode effects. For the background image, I didn't use any effects. However, for the lion in the middle, I applied a mask layer to it and removed the background. Then for the two lions in the background, I applied a mask layer to each of them to also remove the background, and used the lightening blending effect on them, as well as making them less opaque. This way, the main lion with 100% opacity is the main focus, and the two in the background are used to show two lions fighting for something.
