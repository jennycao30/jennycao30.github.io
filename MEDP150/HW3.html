Adobe Camera Raw Features

Shadows For this feature, you get a chance to play with the shadows - how much shadow you like to reduce and/or produce.
Blacks For this feature, you are playing with the color black - given the opportunity to make black more apparent and strong or to reduce it.
Clarity The purpose for this feature is to make the photos more or less sharp. By sliding to the left, you are making the picture less sharp. However, if you slide to the right, you'll be producing a sharper picture.
Vibrance Vibrance is similar to the Saturation feature. However, the different between the two is that this feature is able to differentiate between which colors are already vibrant enough and which aren't and add more to it. When dragging the slider to the right, this feature will add more vibrancy to the dull colors and not so much on the vibrant colors, so the already vibrant colors won't be overpowering. This feature is like a balancing feature amongst the colors.
Saturation The Saturation feature is similar to the vibrance feature. The main purpose of the saturation feature is to add color. However, the difference is, it isn't able to "sense" out the vibrant and dull colors and will just add more color on top of color.
