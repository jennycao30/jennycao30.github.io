Creative Statement

As a child, I grew up watching a lot of anime (Japanese cartoons) and cartoons on TV. It got to a point where I started to draw and doodle for fun. I would try and draw what I watched, I would pick certain things and draw based on the object. Sometimes when I feel extra creative, I try to draw people based on my own creativity and inspiration.

As I got older, I started expanding my hobbies and started to watch more anime and different kinds of cartoons. I started to like different styles of drawing and animation. There are some interesting animations on Netflix where the artwork is amazing, some of them being Castlevania, The Seven Deadly Sins, and Violet Evergarden. However, three of my all time favorite animes that are not on Netflix are One Piece, Haikyu!, and My Hero Academia. The animation, drawing style, and layout of all of the animations I listed are all amazing - I love it.

Not too long ago, I started making flyers for my volunteer program. I knew since I was a child I wanted my future career to have something to do with being creative, anything at all. The opportunity to create flyers for the volunteer event's has sparked a new interest, which leads me to my decision on my major. I want to somehow combine both studio arts and media studies and create cool pieces. I don't know what kind of pieces I want to create, but that is what my journey is going to be - discovering how to combine the two and create cool pieces that I really love and enjoy doing.
